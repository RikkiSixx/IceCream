#DevIceCream

Spontaneous ice cream for digital people

Sometimes, if the mood is right and the sun is shining, digital-types in Leeds meet at an ice cream van in Leeds and make the most of some down-time.

It’s a break from the office, you get to stretch your legs and catch up with friends, and you might even catch a little Vitamin D with your Vitamin 99 (with flake).

Get involved

Soon you'll be able to sign up here and receive an Ice Cream Alert when there’s a gathering looming. But in the meantime, follow on Twitter or keep an eye out for the #DevIceCream hashtag.

Ice cream is better with friends

This is a social thing, so tell your friends about us. Even better, bring them along too.

Share #DevIceCream on your social network of choice, or just tell them in the office!
